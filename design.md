## Visibility Cell

This is a engine resource where any children are not rendered unless inside of the cell (includes meshes, lights and particle emitters).

```bash
Scene
├── Visibility Cell 0
│   ├── Meshes
│   ├── Lights
│   ├── Particle Emitters
│   └── Visibility Cell 4
│       ├── Meshes
│       ├── Lights
│       ├── Particle Emitters
├── Visibility Cell 1
├── Visibility Cell 2
└── Outside
```
